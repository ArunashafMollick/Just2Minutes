Maggi

We can say that this is king brand of this Segment. Maggi is hot favorite among all the children in the country; noodles have come a long way since their introduction in 1983. Consumed in almost every Indian household , Maggi, which was initially considered as a snack, had taken the place of a staple diet in many Indian homes. This brands has faced many controversies like fail in food safety But after so much banned it returned and covered all its market share instantly.
